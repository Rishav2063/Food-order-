Help & FAQ

How do I place an order?

To place an order, browse the food categories, add items to your cart, and click the "Checkout" button. You’ll then be asked for delivery details and payment.

Can I track my order?

Yes. After checkout, you will receive a tracking link via SMS or email. You can also track it in your account under "My Orders."

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept UPI, credit/debit cards, net banking, and cash on delivery (COD) for eligible locations.

How do I cancel or change my order?

You can cancel or edit your order within 5 minutes of placing it by visiting "My Orders" or contacting support.

What if I have an issue with my food?

If your food is missing or incorrect, please reach out to us immediately using the contact details below. We’re here to help!
